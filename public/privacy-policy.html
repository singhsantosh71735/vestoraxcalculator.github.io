VestoraX Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

VestoraX-all financial calculator is designed to help users perform financial calculations such as SIP planning, EMI estimation, retirement planning, goal tracking, and tax-saving estimation.

Information Collection

The app does not require an account for core calculator use. Financial values entered into calculators are used only to generate on-screen results inside the app.

Personal Data

The app does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, banking credentials, or government identification numbers for calculator use.

How Data Is Used

Any information entered into the calculators is used solely for providing the requested calculations and improving the in-app user experience.
